Daniel Galvin

About me

I am a postdoctoral fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn. I completed my PhD at the University of Glasgow, where my supervisor was Dr. Mark Powell. For the first two years of my PhD I was at Durham University. My interests are in topology, specifically in knot theory and manifolds, most often in dimensions 3 and 4. Currently, I am particularly interested in diffeomorphisms and homeomorphisms of 4-manifolds. I co-organise the MPIM low-dimensional topology seminar.
